Our solar system

The planets below are sized based on their actual diameters in relation to one another as well as their respective distances from the sun. Scroll down to make your way towards the sun and learn an interesting fact about each planet on the way. Each planets diameter in KM's and time it takes to orbit the sun are also listed.
