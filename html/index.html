Interesting facts about the planets in our solar system.

Mercury

  • Diameter: 4,879 km
  • Orbit Period: 88 days
  • Special: Mercury does not have any moons or rings.

Venus

  • Diameter: 12,104 km
  • Orbit Period: 225 days
  • Special: Venus rotates in the opposite direction to most other planets.

Earth

  • Diameter: 12,742 km
  • Orbit Period: 365.26 days
  • Special: Earth is the only planet not named after a god.

Mars

  • Diameter: 6,779 km
  • Orbit Period: 1.9 years
  • Special: Mars is home to the tallest mountain in the solar system.

Jupiter

  • Diameter: 139,822 km
  • Orbit Period: 11.9 years
  • Special: Eight spacecraft have visited Jupiter.

Saturn

  • Diameter: 116,464 km
  • Orbit Period: 29.5 years
  • Special: Saturn has the most extensive rings in the solar system.

Uranus

  • Diameter: 50,724 km
  • Orbit Period: 84.0 years
  • Special: Uranus hits the coldest temperatures of any planet.

Neptune

  • Diameter: 49,244 km
  • Orbit Period: 164.8 years
  • Special: Neptune has 14 moons.
